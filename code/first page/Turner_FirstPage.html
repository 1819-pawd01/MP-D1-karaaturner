Over this past summer, I traveled to Bethany Beach. I stayed for a week. My favorite part about going to the beach is walking on the boardwalk and shopping. I also went to Wildwood, New Jersey for a couple weekends. I like Delaware beaches better, though. When I'm at the beach, I usually wake up earlier than usual to get my day started. I also stay up late to see the sunset and walk on the beach at night. If I'm lucky, I get to see some fireworks on the beach. I love seeing the different sizes and colors of the fireworks at each show.

My favorite color is blue. I like all variations of blue. Even my room used to be bright blue. My favorite shades of blue are baby blue or royal blue. We will never be able to get that cat because everyone in my family is allergic, and now my brother has moved out. I'm hoping once he gets his own apartment, he can adapt a cat so I can visit it.
